  • Do not place the battery in the high-temperature environments. Overheating may cause explosion.
  • Do not disassemble, hit, or crush the battery to avoid battery leaks, overheating, or explosion.
  • Do not burn the battery to avoid ﬁre or explosion.
  • In case of deformation, stop using the battery immediately.
  • Stop using the battery if it is exhibiting signs of discoloration, deformation, abnormal overheating, or swelling
  • Keep your device dry. Do not store or use the product and its accessories in a hot and humid environment or near an open fire.

Reading mode

  • This feature is available only on compatible Mi Phones.
  • Reading mode automatically decreases the level of blue light emitted from the screen to make viewing more comfortable for your eyes.
  • Switching to Reading mode:

There are two ways to turn Reading mode on and off:

1. Swipe down from the top of the Home screen to show the Notification shade toggles, then tap the Reading mode toggle.

2. Go to Settings > Display > Reading mode. On the same screen, you can schedule to turn Reading mode on and off automatically and adjust color temperature.

  • Useful tips:

1. 20-20-20 rule: It is recommended to take a look at something located 20 feet away for 20 seconds every 20 minutes.

2. Blinking: To relieve eye dryness, try to close your eyes for 2 seconds, then open them and blink rapidly for 5 seconds.

3. Refocusing: It is a good exercise for your eye muscles to look away from your screen to the farthest place that you can see, followed by focusing on the thumb put 30 cm away in front of your eyes for a few seconds.

4. Eye rolling: Roll your eyes clockwise a few times, then take a break and roll them in an anti-clockwise direction.

5. Palming: Rub your palms together to generate heat before gently pressing them against your eyes for a few seconds.
